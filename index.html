So what is the creative impact tour?

A nationwide free and low-cost creative experience for youth and families, especially those impacted by foster care, incarceration, and community trauma. This 10+ city tour is a storytelling and wellness experiment to center Black joy, emotional healing, and personal expression.

tour host

Brittany Frizzelle- author, educator, and creative wellness coach

each city stop will host

  • A Black-owned bookstore events
  • A free writing workshops at public libraries
  • A creative wellness masterclasses for youth in need

A multi-genre writer, educator, and creative wellness facilitator whose work lives at the intersection of healing and liberation. With a JD/MS in Education and over a decade of community-based teaching, Brittany has led storytelling programs for youth in group homes, reentry programs, and Title 1 schools nationwide.

Her workshops blend ritual, resistance, and joy, offering transformative spaces for writing, reflection, and renewal. She is the author of a poetry collection on mental health, a children’s book on student-teacher bonds, and the host of a podcast exploring creative accountability.
